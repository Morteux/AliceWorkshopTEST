AliceWorkshop
- For experienced players: Search and save your favourite teams, so you can quickly look them up. Check random teams for any content type, or just select a random team of your favourite list if you don't know what to play. Filter by specific archetypes and characters in team. You can look about more detailed info about characters, like their multipliers, stats, ascension material and more... Also, you have access to pre-release characters.
- For new/casuals players: Discover new teams with your favourite characters, including full builds general guides. You will be able to save them and have a briefly description and rotation to try them. You can search teams with your current built/owned characters, being possible to filter with one or more characters. Learn about archetypes, and how to create teams with examples and random teams. You will have also a mini-guide for building characters.
Teams disclaimer
All teams are selected and/or reviewed by our team creator members (yes, they are humans). Therefore, all info displayed by AW (AliceWorkshop) is, to a greater or lesser extent, subjective to the info we have gathered so far and can exists inaccuracies. You can suggest changes and additions in our official Discord channel!
Viabilities disclaimer
Tags are a general guideline not Archon's law, specifically in teams viabilities (recommended, viable, for fun and unique). A team with recommended tag implies that it is the best, or one of the best, teams for a character in that team.
